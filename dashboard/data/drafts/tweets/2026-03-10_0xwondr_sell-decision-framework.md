the hardest problem in trading isn't finding entries. it's that sell decisions are made under completely different psychological conditions than buy decisions.

when you buy, you're evaluating forward expected value. when you sell, you're anchored to your P&L, your peak unrealized, and your identity as someone who "called it."

write your exit thesis at entry. literally the same doc. "I will sell when X, Y, or Z." if none of those conditions are met, you hold. if they are, you sell and don't look back.

citrini just made 13x on AAOI and is in pain because it kept going. that's the game.
