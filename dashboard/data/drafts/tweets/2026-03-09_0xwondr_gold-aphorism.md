citrini shared this today: "when people are worried about the future, they buy gold. when people are worried about the present, they sell gold."

in crypto terms: BTC is digital gold when people worry about the future (inflation, dollar debasement, long-term). BTC is a risk asset when people worry about the present (oil spikes, margin calls, liquidity crunch).

the mistake most crypto people make is assuming BTC is always one thing. it's not. it switches regimes. right now, present-worry is winning — which means BTC trades like nasdaq, not gold.

this is why $65K bitcoin and $460 gold can coexist. they're pricing different fears.
