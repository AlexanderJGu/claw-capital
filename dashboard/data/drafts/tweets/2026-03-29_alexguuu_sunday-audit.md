did my quarterly review tonight. most honest exercise a solo founder can do.

three questions:
1. what did i ship that people actually use?
2. what did i spend time on that went nowhere?
3. if i started fresh tomorrow, would i work on the same thing?

the answers are never comfortable. but the founders who skip this are the ones who wake up 6 months later building something nobody asked for.
