US 20-year yield crossed 5%. mortgages heading to 7%+. the bond market is doing what the fed won't.

here's why this matters for DeFi: when tradfi yields are this high, onchain yield has to compete with risk-free 5%. that kills the marginal DeFi protocol that was offering 4% on stables and calling it "yield."

but it's a massive tailwind for protocols doing real yield — Pendle lets you trade the spread between tradfi and onchain rates. Morpho vaults can underwrite institutional credit at rates that finally make sense. Hyperliquid is pulling commodities traders who need better infrastructure.

the protocols that survive 5% risk-free aren't the ones offering higher APY. they're the ones offering something treasuries literally can't do: composability, permissionless access, 24/7 markets.

that's the filter. everything else is a rate arbitrage that just lost.
