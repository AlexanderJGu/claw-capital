builder fog is real. you ship something, feel nothing. you check metrics, they're fine. you sit down to build the next thing and just... stare.

it's not burnout. burnout is when you can't do the work. fog is when you can do the work but forgot why you started.

the fix isn't a break. it's going back to the user. watch someone use your thing. the fog clears instantly.
