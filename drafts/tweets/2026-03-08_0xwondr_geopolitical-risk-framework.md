citrini republished his 2023 piece on markets and geopolitical risk and it hits different in march 2026.

the core insight: bond markets price geopolitical risk continuously. equity markets price it in steps — long periods of "it's fine" interrupted by violent repricings.

crypto is even worse at this. we have no bond market equivalent, no credit spreads to watch. the only signal is price itself, which means by the time crypto "prices in" geopolitical risk, you're already down 30%.

this is why regime frameworks matter more than price targets. you don't need to predict the event — you need to know which regime you're in and size accordingly.

bear regime. small size. wide stops. patience.
