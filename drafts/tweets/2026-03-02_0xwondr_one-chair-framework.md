the "one chair" framework:

in every narrative trade, there's one asset that actually has a business behind it. the rest are proxies and pretenders riding correlation.

your job in a bear market isn't finding the next narrative. it's identifying which single asset within each narrative deserves your concentrated bet.

VVV vs VIRTUAL vs TAO is the perfect case study. one has 30K API users and weekly buybacks. the others have vibes.

the framework works everywhere: MORPHO vs random lending forks. HYPE vs generic L1s. concentration > diversification when the tide goes out.
