the most important chart in crypto right now isn't a crypto chart. it's oil.

every sustained oil spike above $100 in the last 20 years preceded a risk asset drawdown. 2008, 2022, now.

the mechanism: oil spike → consumer margin compression → earnings miss → multiple compression → liquidity crunch. crypto sits at the end of that chain because it's the most liquidity-sensitive asset class.

BTC bouncing from 65 to 70 while oil sits at 115+ isn't strength. it's latency.

the question for Q2: does oil come back down (venezuela buffer, iran deal, demand destruction) or does it stay elevated? that single variable determines whether this is a bear market rally or the start of something worse.
