the agent economy thesis keeps getting stronger and nobody's building the right infrastructure for it.

current state: agents that hold wallets and make transactions. cool demo. zero real usage.

what's missing: reputation systems (which agents are trustworthy?), budget controls (who authorized this spend?), and dispute resolution (the agent bought the wrong thing, now what?).

every one of these is a protocol-level opportunity. not another agent launchpad — the actual plumbing that makes agent commerce work.

the 2020 DeFi builders who moved into AI understand this. the AI lab people don't. that's the alpha.
